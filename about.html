A quick introduction

Curious, careful, and driven by problems that matter.

I'm a researcher based at IIT Ropar. My work sits at the junction of theory and practice — I enjoy pulling apart a hard problem, finding the core question underneath, and building something principled that solves it.

Over the years I've contributed to peer-reviewed venues, mentored students, and collaborated with teams across academia and industry. I care about reproducible research, clear writing, and work that holds up outside the lab.

Outside research, I enjoy reading, mentoring early-career researchers, and exploring new ideas across disciplines.
